Breakfast Casserole

Description

When I was only four years old, I rode my bike without training wheels for the first time, and that gave me the confidence to cook.

This deliciously cheesy and tasty breakfast casserole is filling and the perfect start to your day, or an amazing treat for a Christmas morning!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sausage
  • 20 ounces (half a bag) of frozen shredded hashbrowns (these can be frozen or thawed when assembling)
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper chopped
  • 1 poblano pepper chopped (optional)
  • 8 eggs
  • 1-1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1-1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Grease a 9x13 baking dish
  3. Pour half the bag of hashbrowns into the baking dish in an even layer
  4. Cook onions and peppers over medium-low heat and set aside
  5. Over medium heat, brown the sausage crumbling as it cooks
  6. Sprinkle cheese over the hashbrowns
  7. Combine the peppers,onion, and sausage then pour mixture over the cheese and hashbrowns in the dish
  8. Whisk eggs, heavy cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together in a bowl then pour over the hashbrown and sausage.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 375 for 45 minutes
  10. Uncover and cook an additional 10-15 minutes

    11. This can be made the night before, after you cover in foil it will be good in the fridge overnight
